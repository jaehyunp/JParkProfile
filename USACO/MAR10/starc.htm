USACO MAR10 STARC example

The x-axis is S_1/S_3, and the y-axis is S_2/S_3.
The blue polygon in the middle represents the set of feasible solutions.
There are two lines strictly crossing the polygon, and one horizontal line touching the polygon.
All of these lines correspond to undecidable cases.
Other lines don't cross the polygon, therefore the answers to these lines are either "J" or "B".
